Compre Agora!
*10000Pa powerful suction fan blower: Data tested by the manufacturer. With a full charge and in turbo mode, the robot vacuum can achieve suction of up to 10,000Pa. Results may vary due to different usage conditions.
*5200mAh is the nominal battery capacity.
*180-minute: Data tested by the manufacturer. A sample robot vacuum was charged to full and tested on an open tile surface at different power levels. The working time was recorded until the robot vacuum initiated its return and the total cleaning area was displayed in the Xiaomi Home app. Under these conditions, the robot can vacuum and mop for ≥180 minutes in standard mode with a single run. Actual performance may vary depending on usage conditions, floor type and other factors.
*90 days: Data tested by the manufacturer. Household rubbish was evenly strewn across a selected 20m² open tiled area. In a simulated home environment where vacuuming was done twice a day, the device lasted 180 cycles. This is sufficient for about 90 days of dust collection for a typical household. Actual number of days may vary depending on factors such as home size and environment.
*Anti-tangle: Data tested by the manufacturer. Means that the anti-tangling main brush installed on the robot vacuum can effectively reduce hair entanglement during vacuuming. The actual anti-tangling performance may vary in different environments.
*4L dust bag: Data tested by the manufacturer. The robot vacuum was fully charged and used to suck plastic pellets until it could no longer suck anything up. The actual maximum capacity was ≥4L. Actual results may vary due to differences in usage conditions.
*210mL water tank: Data tested by the manufacturer. Water tank capacity was measured with a measuring cup to be ≥210mL. Actual results may vary due to differences in usage conditions.
*20mm: Data tested by the manufacturer. When the mop pad is dry, the robot vacuum can overcome steps with a height of up to 20mm. Actual results may vary due to different usage conditions.
*Xiaomi Home app (V6.7.200) is currently only compatible with iOS10.0 and above. For the best user experience, please update your device to the latest software version.
*Unless otherwise specified, all data and statistic figures on this page come from Xiaomi Internal Labs.
